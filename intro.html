Welcome to the Dice Roll Game!

This game lets you experience the excitement of rolling dice with customizable options for different-sided dice. Your task is to predict and place markers on the sums you believe will appear the most when two dice are rolled.

How to Play:

  1. Select the number of sides for each die (6 to 20 sides).
  2. Place up to 10 markers on any sum that two dice can roll (e.g., 7, 8, etc.).
  3. Click "Roll Dice" to throw the dice.
  4. The roll counter will track how many dice rolls you've taken.
  5. If the sum of the two dice matches a number with markers, one marker is removed from that number.
  6. The game continues until all markers have been removed.

Good luck, and may the dice be ever in your favor!
